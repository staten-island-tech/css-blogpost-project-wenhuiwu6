My Most Played Games

Identity V

Offical Website Identity V is a free-to-play, 1v4 asymmetrical survival horror game where a single Hunter tries to capture four Survivors, who must decode cipher machines and escape through the exit gates. The game features a mystery narrative, told from the perspective of Detective Orpheus investigating a string of strange events at the Oletus Manor, which involves experiments and a deadly game where participants' desires and fears are exploited.

Genshin Impact

The plot of Genshin Impact follows the Traveler, an interstellar wanderer who arrives in the fantasy world of Teyvat with their twin sibling but is separated from them by a mysterious deity, the "Unknown God". The Traveler awakens years later with no memory of their twin's fate and, accompanied by their guide Paimon, sets out to explore Teyvat's seven nations, each ruled by an elemental Archon, to find their sibling and uncover the world's many mysteries.

Honkai: Star Rail

In Honkai: Star Rail, you play as the amnesiac Trailblazer who boards the Astral Express, a space train, and joins a diverse crew on an interplanetary adventure. The core plot involves traveling to different worlds to resolve crises caused by powerful objects called Stellarons and opposing various cosmic threats like the Antimatter Legion, all while unraveling the mysteries of your own past and the Stellaron Hunters, a mysterious organization that also seeks Stellarons for their own enigmatic goals.
