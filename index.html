Identity V

Identity V is a free-to-play, asymmetrical survival horror game where one Hunter chases four Survivors. Survivors must decode five cipher machines and open the exit gates to escape the map, while the Hunter's goal is to prevent their escape and eliminate them using rocket chairs. The game's overarching story follows Detective Orpheus's investigation into the mysterious Oletus Manor, where he uncovers a series of dark experiments and games orchestrated by the manor's owners, with players often unknowingly participating in recreations of these past events.

Genshin Impact

The plot of Genshin Impact follows the Traveler, an otherworldly being, who is separated from their twin sibling by an "Unknown God" while trying to leave the world of Teyvat. The Traveler awakens years later with a floating companion named Paimon and begins a journey across Teyvat's seven nations, each ruled by an elemental god known as an Archon, to find their lost sibling and uncover the world's many mysteries. Along the way, the Traveler gets involved in local affairs, learns about the history of the world, and confronts ancient powers like the Abyss Order and the Heavenly Principles

Honkai: Star Rail

In Honkai: Star Rail, you play as the amnesiac Trailblazer who boards the Astral Express, a space train, and joins a diverse crew on an interplanetary adventure. The core plot involves traveling to different worlds to resolve crises caused by powerful objects called Stellarons and opposing various cosmic threats like the Antimatter Legion, all while unraveling the mysteries of your own past and the Stellaron Hunters, a mysterious organization that also seeks Stellarons for their own enigmatic goals
